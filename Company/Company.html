About Company

Getting to Know Geocraft Nusantara

Geocraft Nusantara GeoCraft Nusantara is a business entity specializing in geospatial solutions, surveying, mapping, and technical studies. The company exists to support efficient, transparent, and sustainable spatial and natural resource planning and management. Using a geospatial technology-based approach and systematic spatial analysis methodology, GeoCraft Nusantara provides accurate data and information to support more informed decision-making in development planning, disaster risk mitigation, and environmental management.

Supported by a team of professionals competent in GIS, field surveys, mapping, and technical studies, GeoCraft Nusantara provides a variety of services ranging from field data collection, digital mapping, spatial analysis, to the preparation of studies and recommendations based on geospatial data. With a commitment to quality, accuracy, and professional service, the company is ready to be a trusted partner for government agencies, the private sector, and other institutions requiring reliable geospatial solutions and mapping data.

With the support of professional staff and the use of the latest mapping technology, we provide services that are adaptive, efficient, and relevant to our clients' needs.
